Problem Statement

There are new students, faculty, and visitors who get lost on their first day visiting ODU because they are not familiar with the campus. For instance, students who commute that miss orientation currently do not have a good way to learn the layout of the campus without making the trip to ODU.

Customer Base

Old Dominion University Students, Faculty, Staff, and Visitors

Societal Problems
  • Newcomers on campus do not know the layout of campus that well.
  • Newcomers could use a way to learn all of the building names.
  • Upcoming events on campus can result in difficulty travelling on campus.
  • Newcomers do not know how long it can take walking from one building to another.
Solution Goals
  • Provide a 3D map of the ODU campus to explore.
  • Specify the buildings on campus.
  • Provide parking lot information.
  • Provide routes an individual can take to get from place to place on campus.
